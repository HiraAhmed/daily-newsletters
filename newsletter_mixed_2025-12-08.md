### Ethereum's ZKsync Lite ZK-rollup to Retire by 2026

**Summary:** 
ZKsync Lite, Ethereum's first ZK-rollup, is set to retire in 2026 after serving as a proof-of-concept for zero-knowledge rollups. ### Ethereum On-Chain Gas Futures May Stabilize Fees
**Source:** [https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/693646229be9f8297a3d0a19/](https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/693646229be9f8297a3d0a19/)

**Summary:** 
Ethereum plans to introduce an on-chain gas futures market to stabilize transaction fees, allowing users to purchase gas at predetermined rates for future periods. This mechanism aims to shield users from price volatility during peak demand times and provide a transparent platform for anticipating and hedging future gas prices. This system is expected to reduce the unpredictability of gas fees and foster a more stable operational environment for Ethereum network users.
