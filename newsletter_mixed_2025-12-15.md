Exor, the holding company owned by the Agnelli family, has rejected a takeover bid from Tether for Juventus Football Club, emphasizing that the club's historical values and legacy are not for sale. ### YO Labs Secures $10M for Crypto Yield Optimization
**Source:** [https://cryptonews.net/news/defi/32135060/](https://cryptonews.net/news/defi/32135060/)

**Summary:** 
YO Labs has secured $10 million in a Series A funding round to enhance its cross-chain crypto yield optimization protocol, YO Protocol. The company aims to expand its platform to support more blockchains and enhance its infrastructure, facilitating users in earning yields on crypto assets through automatic rebalancing across multiple DeFi protocols. YO Protocol calculates 'Risk Adjusted Yield' by assessing thousands of risk vectors, such as protocol age and code audit history, to determine the probability of default, enhancing security by minimizing reliance on bridges.
