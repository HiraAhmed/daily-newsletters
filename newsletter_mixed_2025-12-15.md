### Nansen CEO Warns Ethereum May Be Obsolete by 2030
**Source:** [https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/693f8eb9601b676959ed95e6/](https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/693f8eb9601b676959ed95e6/)

**Summary:** 
Alex Svanevik, CEO of Nansen, warns of potential obsolescence of Ethereum by 2030 due to critical issues such as scalability, speed, high transaction fees, and network congestion. Svanevik calls for a cultural shift within the Ethereum ecosystem towards embracing data-driven problem-solving and constructive criticism, emphasizing the importance of confronting challenging data, prioritizing user experience, and accelerating scalability solutions. Competing networks are making significant inroads by focusing on decentralized physical infrastructure and enhancing user experiences, areas where Ethereum is currently lagging.
    
### Juventus Owner Declines Tether's Takeover Offer
**Source:** [https://cointelegraph.com/news/juventus-owner-exor-unanimously-rejects-tether-takeover](https://cointelegraph.com/news/juventus-owner-exor-unanimously-rejects-tether-takeover)

**Summary:** 
Exor, the holding company owned by the Agnelli family, has rejected a substantial takeover bid from Tether for Juventus Football Club, emphasizing that the club's historical values and legacy are not for sale. Tether's offer, exceeding $1 billion for a controlling 65.4% stake, was aimed at acquiring all shares of Juventus, with plans to make a public offer for the remaining shares at the same price. Exor's decision aligns with its commitment to support Juventus' new management and strategic direction to achieve strong results both on and off the field.
    
### YO Labs Secures $10M for Crypto Yield Optimization
**Source:** [https://cryptonews.net/news/defi/32135060/](https://cryptonews.net/news/defi/32135060/)

**Summary:** 
YO Labs has secured $10 million in a Series A funding round to enhance its cross-chain crypto yield optimization protocol, YO Protocol. YO Protocol enables dynamic capital allocation across its multi-chain vaults to areas offering the most favorable risk-adjusted yields, calculating 'Risk Adjusted Yield' by assessing thousands of risk vectors. This architecture mitigates bridge-related security risks and employs a 'DeFi Graph' to manage active risks during market volatility or protocol failures, ensuring safer and more reliable yield optimization.
