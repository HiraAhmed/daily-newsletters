### Buterin's 4-Year Plan for Faster, Quantum-Proof Ethereum
**Source:** [https://cointelegraph.com/news/vitalik-details-roadmap-for-faster-quantum-resistant-ethereum](https://cointelegraph.com/news/vitalik-details-roadmap-for-faster-quantum-resistant-ethereum)

**Summary:** 
Vitalik Buterin, Ethereum's co-founder, has detailed a comprehensive four-year roadmap aimed at enhancing Ethereum's efficiency and security, including making it quantum-proof. The roadmap introduces significant reductions in slot time, which will improve the network's responsiveness and transaction confirmation times. Additionally, the plan includes a major overhaul of the finality system, reducing the time it takes for transactions to become irreversible and transitioning to a simpler, quantum-resistant system. The roadmap, which includes seven planned forks approximately every six months, also highlights the Ethereum Foundation's focus on quantum readiness and gas limits as priorities for 2026.
