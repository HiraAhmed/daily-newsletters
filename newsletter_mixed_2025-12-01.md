### Upbit Hack Linked to Lazarus Group, Seoul Investigates
**Source:** [https://decrypt.co/350303/upbit-hack-north-koreas-lazarus-seoul-opens-probe](https://decrypt.co/350303/upbit-hack-north-koreas-lazarus-seoul-opens-probe)

**Summary:** 
South Korea's largest cryptocurrency exchange, Upbit, experienced a security breach involving its Solana hot wallet, resulting in a loss of approximately $36 million. The breach is suspected to be the work of North Korea's Lazarus Group. In response to the breach, Upbit's operator, Dunamu, froze the affected wallets, transferred remaining funds to more secure cold wallets, and assured full reimbursement to the impacted customers. South Korean authorities have initiated a formal investigation, including an on-site probe at Upbit. Dunamu is set to be acquired by Naver Financial in a major all-stock deal valued at $10.29 billion, pending regulatory approval.
