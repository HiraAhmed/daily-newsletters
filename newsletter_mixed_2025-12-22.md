### Uniswap to Burn 100M UNI Tokens After Proposal
**Source:** [https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/6948fdf86b9a2d284866c87e/](https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/6948fdf86b9a2d284866c87e/)

**Summary:** 
The Uniswap community has approved the 'UNIfication' governance proposal, which will introduce a mechanism for burning UNI tokens using trading fee revenue and will remove 100 million UNI tokens from the protocol’s treasury. The proposal also plans to implement Protocol Fee Discount Auctions, transfer certain responsibilities from the Uniswap Foundation to Uniswap Labs, and set a growth budget of 20 million UNI per year. Despite a 25% increase in UNI price after voting began, the token has seen slight declines amid broader market challenges.

### Klarna Teams Up with Coinbase for Stablecoin Funding
**Source:** [https://cointelegraph.com/news/klarna-partners-coinbase-usdc-institutional-funding](https://cointelegraph.com/news/klarna-partners-coinbase-usdc-institutional-funding)

**Summary:** 
Swedish fintech Klarna has partnered with Coinbase to incorporate stablecoin, specifically USDC, into its institutional funding strategies, enabling access to a new class of institutional investors. Klarna has also launched its own US dollar-pegged stablecoin, KlarnaUSD, on the Tempo blockchain, which is currently in the testnet phase with plans for a mainnet launch in 2026. Klarna's CFO, Niclas Neglén, sees the potential of stablecoins to revolutionize funding mechanisms but also acknowledges the regulatory, market, and operational risks involved.
