### Kazakhstan to Boost Cryptocurrency Reserves by 2026
**Source:** [https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/6911714449317946da92e72a/](https://coinmarketcap.com/community/articles/6911714449317946da92e72a/)

**Summary:** 
Kazakhstan plans to establish a national cryptocurrency reserve by 2026, valued between $500 million to $1 billion, using assets from state-conducted mining and confiscated wealth. The initiative is part of a broader strategy to integrate digital assets into the country's economic framework, investing in exchange-traded products and blockchain enterprises. The National Bank of Kazakhstan plans to utilize seized Bitcoins to build this reserve. Recent legislative updates have improved the regulatory environment for crypto exchanges and allowed transactions involving payment cards and stablecoins.
